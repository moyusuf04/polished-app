# Product Requirements

## Core Features
1. **AI-Generated Micro-Lesson Feed:** Curated lessons (~300 words) with quick facts and 2–3 "Conversational Hooks."
2. **The <200-Character "Reflection Box":** A mandatory input field at the end of a lesson to practice critical thinking.
3. **Peer Response Reveal:** A blind reveal showing 3 peer reflections after the user submits their own.
4. **Anonymous Guest Mode:** Allow 3 lesson completions before requiring email signup.

## Target Audience
Ambitious young professionals or students who want to hold their own in social and professional discussions but lack early exposure.

## Success Metrics (30 Days)
- **Completion Rate:** 70% of started lessons
- **Day 2 Retention:** 25%
- **Response Rate:** 50% of lesson finishers
